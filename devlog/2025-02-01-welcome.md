I scraped the top 2,000 LeetCode users' profile pictures and ran them through a YOLOv5 anime-face detection model to see if higher-ranked programmers are more likely to have anime PFPs. Turns out they are — the ratio trends upward as rank increases, peaking at 26% in the 300–400 range. Correlation doesn't imply causation, but it was a fun weekend project.
